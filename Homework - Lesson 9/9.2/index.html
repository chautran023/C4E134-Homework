How to Use:
    :) Click "Edit" button to edit tasks. When done editing, click "Done" to update.
    :) Click the task title to mark "Complete". Click again to unmark "Complete".
    :) Click "Sort" to arrange the tasks alphabetically.
    :) To clear all completed tasks, click "Delete".
    :) To see your completed tasks, click "Show COMPLETED".
    :) To see your unfinished tasks, click "Show UNFINISHED".
    :) To see all your tasks, click "Show ALL".
